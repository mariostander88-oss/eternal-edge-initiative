## 2. Competitive Advantage
By manufacturing locally within the SADC region, we eliminate significant overhead costs:
* **No trans-oceanic shipping of raw ore:** We process near the source.
* **Duty-Free Trade:** Utilizing SADC trade agreements for moving Ni and Cu across borders.
* **Currency Arbitrage:** Sourcing inputs in ZAR/local currencies while selling finished tools in USD.

## 3. Risk Mitigation
* **Supply Volatility:** Zirconium supply is diversified between SA west coast and Namibia to prevent single-point bottlenecks.
* **Energy Security:** Manufacturing facility to include solar supplementation (Solar PV) to mitigate load shedding risks during the melting phase.
